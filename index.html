Supplement Stack

No stimulant-stacking BS

Keep

  • Zoloft as prescribed
  • Adderall as prescribed
  • Creatine
  • Omega-3
  • Multivitamin
  • CoQ10
  • Berberine 500mg 2x/day
  • Electrolytes
  • Glycine
  • Apigenin
  • Phosphatidylserine
  • Trazodone as prescribed

Add / Upgrade

  • Magnesium Glycinate 300–400mg at night
  • Ashwagandha KSM-66 600mg at night
  • Green Tea Extract EGCG 300–500mg in morning
  • Vitamin D3 + K2 if not already covered
  • Zinc 15–30mg if not already covered

Remove / Limit

  • Remove NALT AM
  • Remove NALT PM
  • Alpha-GPC max 1x/day optional
  • Caffeine max 150–200mg
  • No caffeine after 2PM
  • No random testosterone boosters

Medication Note

Because you use Zoloft, Adderall, and Trazodone, do not keep adding stimulant or mood-affecting supplements without clearing it with your prescriber.

Dealer Meal System

Protein first. Carbs controlled.
Core Rule: Protein first. Control carbs. Moderate fats. No junk stacking.

Breakfast Options

  • Eggs + egg whites
  • Turkey sausage
  • Greek yogurt
  • Steel-cut oats
  • Whole-grain toast
  • Berries
  • Avocado
  • Chia seeds
  • Almond butter

Lunch Orders

  • Chipotle/Qdoba: bowl, double chicken or steak, light rice, fajita veggies, pico, skip sour cream
  • Jimmy John’s/Sub shop: unwich or whole grain, double meat, avocado, no heavy mayo
  • Chick-fil-A: grilled nuggets or grilled sandwich, fruit or salad, skip fries unless planned
  • Restaurant: grilled protein, vegetables, side rice or potato, light oil/butter

Office Snacks

  • Low-sugar protein bars
  • Beef jerky
  • Greek yogurt
  • Cottage cheese
  • Almonds or walnuts
  • String cheese
  • Protein shake
  • Apple + peanut butter
  • Hummus + carrots

Dinner Rules

  • Protein first
  • Vegetables always
  • Small carb if needed
  • No pasta-heavy meals
  • No fried food
  • No sugary drinks
  • After 9PM: protein only if needed
