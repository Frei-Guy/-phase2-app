🔥 Dealer Cut System

Built for a 9:00 AM–8:30 PM dealership schedule with a 30-minute drive home. Goal: lose beer belly, reduce chest fat, improve morning energy, control stress/cortisol, and stay sharp.

Schedule Stack Meals Workouts Macros KPIs Rules

Daily Dealer Schedule

6:15 AM

Wake + Hydrate

Supplements: Electrolytes

Food: 16–20 oz water, optional lemon

Get light exposure immediately. This anchors cortisol rhythm and helps morning energy.

6:30–7:00 AM

Workout

Workout: Strength or cardio depending on day.

Keep it 30 minutes. Consistency beats destroying yourself.

7:30 AM

Breakfast + AM Stack

Supplements: Zoloft, Adderall AM, Creatine, Omega-3, Multivitamin, CoQ10, Green Tea Extract.

Food: Eggs, turkey sausage, Greek yogurt, oats, berries, toast, avocado, chia seeds, almond butter.

Remove: NALT. Limit: Alpha-GPC max 1x/day. Caffeine max 150–200mg.

8:30 AM

Commute

No extra caffeine. Hydrate. Get mentally ready for prime deal hours.

9:00 AM–12:00 PM

Prime Deal Hours

No stimulant stacking. Keep water nearby. Don’t chase energy with more pills.

12:15 PM

Lunch

Supplements: Berberine 500mg

Food: Protein + controlled carbs + vegetables.

Do not go too low-carb here or you will crash later.

12:30 PM

Midday Focus

Supplements: Adderall PM if prescribed/needed.

No NALT. Avoid extra caffeine unless absolutely necessary.

2:30 PM

Crash Prevention

Food: Protein bar, cottage cheese, almonds, walnuts, beef jerky, Greek yogurt, string cheese.

Protein/fat snack prevents the 4PM crash.

5:30–6:30 PM

Dealer Dinner

Supplements: Berberine 500mg

Food: Chicken, fish, lean beef, tofu, broccoli, asparagus, spinach, sweet potato, rice, barley.

This is your most important fat-loss meal. Protein first. Keep carbs controlled.

8:30 PM

Leave Work

Start shutdown mode. No caffeine. No hype music. No work rage spiral.

8:30–9:00 PM

Drive Home

Commute becomes decompression. Calm audio. Dim brightness. Tell your brain the workday is done.

9:00 PM

Arrive Home

If hungry: Greek yogurt, turkey slices, casein shake.

No junk. No carbs unless planned.

9:15 PM

Night Stack

Supplements: Magnesium Glycinate, Ashwagandha KSM-66, Glycine, Apigenin, Phosphatidylserine, Trazodone.

Main recovery, cortisol, and sleep stack.

10:15 PM

Sleep

Target 7.5–8 hours. Sleep is the fat-loss multiplier.

1900–2100Calories

Aggressive Cut Rules: No alcohol. No cheat meals during the week. Carbs mainly breakfast and lunch. Dinner = protein + vegetables. Night food = protein only if needed.

KPI Tracker

Track Weekly

  • Daily scale weight, use weekly average
  • Waist measurement 1x/week at belly button
  • Front and side mirror photos 2x/week
  • Energy score 1–10 daily
  • Compliance percentage: diet, workouts, steps, sleep

Adjustment Rules

  • If weight is not dropping, reduce calories by 200/day.
  • If energy crashes, aggressive cut may be too hard.
  • If waist is shrinking, do not change anything.
  • If workouts suffer badly, increase carbs around breakfast/lunch.
  • If sleep sucks, remove late caffeine/stimulation first.

Hard Rules

No Primal Viking/testosterone booster.
No alcohol during aggressive cut.
No late-night carbs.
No caffeine after 2PM.
No NALT while using Adderall.
Protein target is non-negotiable.
Sleep is the fat-loss multiplier.
8,000–10,000 steps/day.
10–15 minute walk after dinner whenever possible.
If you go off plan, next meal is clean protein + vegetables. No “restart Monday” BS.
